# Employment Agreement

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is between **Acme Holdings LLC** (the "Employer") and **Jane Smith** (the "Employee").

## Start and compensation
Employment commences on **January 9, 2024**.
Base salary is **$120,000 per year**.

## Benefits
Employee is eligible for benefits under the Employer's benefit plans subject to plan terms.

## Termination and ROE
Employer may terminate employment without cause on written notice.
The Employer will issue a Record of Employment after an interruption of earnings in accordance with applicable law.
